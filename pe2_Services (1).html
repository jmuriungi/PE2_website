At Precious Elders 2 AFH we cherish our clients and provide services selflessly and with patience and kindness. We value the health, nutrition and hygiene of our clients and provide the following services while always respecting their wishes and treating them with diginty.

  • Healthy and nutritionous home cooked meals with the occasional Kenyan dish.
  • Blood glucose monitoring for diabetic clients.
  • Hygiene care including baths, nail care, incontinence management, denture care and more.
  • Transportation to doctors appointments and other transport accomodations as needed.
  • Medication assistance.
